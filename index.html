About VaultDeals

VaultDeals.io tracks prices across every major authorized PC game retailer so you always know whether right now is actually a good time to buy. We score every deal against its historical low — A+ means you're at or within 5% of the all-time lowest price ever recorded.

We built this because buying a game at full price and watching it drop 60% two weeks later is genuinely frustrating. VaultDeals exists so that doesn't happen to you anymore.

What we do

  • Track live prices across 12+ authorized stores
  • Score deals A+ through C+ based on historical pricing data
  • Surface games at or near their all-time lows
  • Show free game giveaways from Epic, Steam, and GOG
  • Never list grey market or unauthorized key sellers

VaultDeals is independent. We're not affiliated with any of the stores we track.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

VaultDeals.io does not collect personal data simply from visiting the site. We do not use tracking pixels, behavioral analytics, or fingerprinting of any kind.

If you sign up for price alerts, your email address is stored solely to send you notifications. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email — this permanently removes your address from our system.

Cookies and local storage

We use browser localStorage for session preferences only. No advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies are set by VaultDeals.

Third-party services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). As a result, Google may log your IP address as part of serving font files. You can block fonts.googleapis.com in your browser and the site will fall back to system fonts.

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare logs request metadata at the infrastructure level as standard practice. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.

When you click through to a store, that store may set its own cookies. Price data is provided by GG.deals and free game data by GamerPower.
